Budgeting Tips
Budgeting Guidelines
  • Track your income and expenses for a month to understand your spending patterns
  • Allocate funds to different categories (food, utilities, entertainment, etc.)
  • Aim to save at least 20% of your income
  • Review and adjust your budget regularly
Common Budget Categories
  • Housing: 25-35% of income
  • Food: 10-15% of income
  • Transportation: 10-15% of income
  • Utilities: 5-10% of income
  • Savings: 15-20% of income
  • Personal/Discretionary: 5-10% of income
